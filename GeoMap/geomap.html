How it works

- GeoMap -
Geomap is an app that displays data/information about what lies beneath the surface in a 3D digital manner.Geomap delivers the location of pipes, underground cables and other utilities beneath the surface. Delivering up to date accurate information to anyone doing construction/work that involves digging or excavating.

- Team -
Software Developer
 The primary role of the software developers will be to write and test code, assess the suitability of the coding software we will use and to diagnose any faults with the code.

Software Designer
 The primary role of the software designers will be to create the foundation that our software will be built on using research, design and planning techniques.

Project Manager
 The primary role of the project manager is to control risks and to minimise uncertainty with any project we undertake by ensuring we adhere to a policy of open communication that allows everyone to express their opinions and concerns.

Software Architect
 The primary role of the software architect will be to make the final design choices and enforce all the technical standards.

Quality Assurance Specialist
 The quality assurance specialist will ensure that the final product observes the company's high-quality standards.

Business Analyst
 The job of the business analyst is to understand the needs of the customer and to gather the information that will help better their experience.
